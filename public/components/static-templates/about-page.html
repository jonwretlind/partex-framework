About Us

Who we are

We are Partex, a family-owned company with 75 years of experience in industrial marking systems. Our world-leading solutions enhance safety and boost productivity for electricians worldwide. Electrification is the future, and we're driving progress. With care for our customers, each other, and generations to come, we're shaping a safer and more sustainable electric future.

Core values

Partex is a multigenerational Company with a rich tradition. From the very beginning, the owners built the brand based on basic human values. For over 75 years, countless Employees have found that good relationships, mutual trust, and reliable quality - these are what give the Company and its people strength. We have particularly experienced this in recent months. Pneumatics

Pneumatic technology has become so common that it is practically used in every modern plant. Our range includes many options for marking pneumatic lines and actuators. We manufacture flexible markers, to fit standard sizes of pneumatic pipes, and labels resistant to chemicals which are perfect for pneumatic actuators in wash down applications.
