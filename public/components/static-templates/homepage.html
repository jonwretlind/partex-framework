Who we are

We are Partex, a family-owned company with 75 years of experience in industrial marking systems. Our world-leading solutions enhance safety and boost productivity for electricians worldwide. Electrification is the future, and we're driving progress. With care for our customers, each other, and generations to come, we're shaping a safer and more sustainable electric future.

Core values

Partex is a multigenerational Company with a rich tradition. From the very beginning, the owners built the brand based on basic human values. For over 75 years, countless Employees have found that good relationships, mutual trust, and reliable quality - these are what give the Company and its people strength. We have particularly experienced this in recent months. Words of support and encouragement from the owners and the Management Board have allowed us to get through this difficult time in terms of security.Seeing the importance and meaning of the words QUALITY, LONG-TERM, CLOSENESS - and their real impact on our life and work - we wish to express them today in our own way, and promote them within our Organisation, and also among our Trade Partners.
